    System Architecture

    Backend data ingestionNode.js fetches USGS earthquake GeoJSON and Open-Meteo weather data.
    Dijkstra routingServer builds graph nodes and edges, then computes shortest hub-to-crisis routes.
    Edmonds-Karp max-flowAllocates finite rescue capacity through constrained graph edges.
    Student risk modelThe dashboard estimates risk, confidence, resource pressure, and dispatch urgency from incident and route features.
